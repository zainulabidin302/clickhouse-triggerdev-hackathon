## The theme

One week. One theme. Build something real.

Build a **chat agent that changes how people interact with information**.

- **Trigger.dev** handles orchestration and background jobs
- **ClickHouse** powers the real-time data layer underneath

Both are required — that's the point. Free cloud credits for both platforms on acceptance.

First-ever virtual hackathon co-hosted by ClickHouse and Trigger.dev, open to professional developers across NAMER and EMEA. The target is a production-quality AI agent chat experience.

## Eligibility

- Professional developers, 18+
- Based in the US, Canada, UK, or an EU country
- Solo or teams of up to 5
- Application-based — looking for builders who have shipped real things and can work independently with minimal hand-holding
- ClickHouse and Trigger.dev employees are **not** eligible
- Teams submit together under **one captain**

---

## Submission rules

- Public **GitHub repo** (or hosted in a cloud environment)
- **Demo video on YouTube**, max 5 minutes
- **Meaningful use of both** ClickHouse and Trigger.dev
- All code must be written **during the build window**
- AI coding assistants (Copilot, Cursor, Claude, ChatGPT) are **explicitly permitted and encouraged**
